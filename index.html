Frequently Asked Questions

You might be getting inquisitive about the event so here is a list of FAQs for your convenience!

What type of payments do you accept ?

We accept cash payment as offline medium, and Google pay or paytm as online mediums.

Is there ID requirement to enter the event ?

Yes an ID proof will be required for the entry in the event. It can be Aadhar card, College ID, Pan ID etc.

How will we get our registration slips if we register online ?

You'll be provided a serial number at the time of registration. You can collect the registration slips from the desk on the day of the event.

Will you take care of our travel expenses ?

We will not be covering travel expenses although coupons might be provided for travel.

Do you offer refunds ?

Sorry, the fee is non refundable. However, in case of medical or any other comparable reason it will be taken into consideration.

What is the registration fee?

Registration fee is Rs 200 per participant for Thapar students and Rs 250 per participant for Non - Thapar students

How many meals will be provided ?

Five to six meals will be provided to the participants which will include a lunch, a dinner, two to three time snacks and a breakfast on the second day.

What if I've never been to a hackathon before ?

Then we're so glad HACKOWASP will be your first ever! We expect you to have some programming or technical experience, but it's not a requirement. We'll have talks, mentors and workshops to help you with your project.

What is the date, time and venue of the HACKOWASP 2.O ?

The HACKOWASP 2.O will be organised in Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala starting from 1st February 2020 to 2nd February 2020.

What is the minimum and maximum number of members per team ?

Minimum number of members per team is four and maximum is six.
